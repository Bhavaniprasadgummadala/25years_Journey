Amma & Nanna

A love that carried a family on its shoulders

25

Some love stories are not written in poetry.

They are written in sacrifice, in silence, in the tears that children never saw — and in a stubborn, beautiful refusal to give up.

6 May 2001

The Beginning of Everything

✦

Two souls stood before God and family, and said — "I choose you."

✦

She tied her life to his with flowers in her hair and hope in her heart.

✦

He promised her a life — not knowing how much courage that promise would demand.

✦

That day, a family was not just formed — it was born.

2001 — 2003

When Dreams Met Reality

✧

The road wasn't paved with gold — it was rough, unlit, and long.

✧

But they walked it — together — never letting the other fall alone.

✧

Every prayer they offered was not for themselves — it was for each other.

4 July 2004

A Cry That Changed Everything

✦

On a summer morning, the world gave them its greatest gift.

✦

A baby's cry filled the room, and two hearts broke open with joy.

✦

That night, Nanna held his firstborn and understood — everything he worked for now had a face.

✦

Amma became a mother — and she was made for it.

2005 — 2007

Hyderabad — The Big Dream

✦

They packed their lives and moved to the city — for a better tomorrow.

✦

Nanna opened a shop with sweat, savings, and a stubborn dream.

✦

Business grew. Life smiled. For a while — the city felt like home.

✦

Amma ran the home like a temple — warm, quiet, full of love.

14 December 2008

The Second Blessing

✦

God blessed them again — another heartbeat joined their home.

✦

Their family was complete. Two sons. One dream. No limits.

✦

That December was the warmest winter they had ever felt.

2009 — 2012

The Test No Parent Asks For

✧

The little one's heart — the one that held so much love — needed help.

✧

Hospital visits became their routine. Prayers became their breath.

✧

To save their child, they sold the shop — without a second thought.

✧

The dream they built with their hands — they let it go with open hands.

✧

Because for Amma and Nanna — no dream is worth more than their child's heartbeat.

2013 — 2019

Rising, Again

✦

Nanna hit the road — marketing, selling, surviving — for his family.

✦

There were good days and terrible days, but he never stopped showing up.

✦

Every rupee earned was quietly placed at the altar of their children's education.

✦

Their struggles? They hid them — so beautifully — behind morning tiffin and bedtime stories.

✦

The children never knew the weight their parents carried — that was the plan.

2020

When the World Stopped

✧

COVID did not just lock the world — it forced impossible choices.

✧

They returned to their hometown — and the whispers followed.

✧

"They couldn't make it in Hyderabad," said the ones who never tried.

✧

But Amma and Nanna did not leave Hyderabad because they failed.

✧

They left because their children's safety mattered more than anyone's opinion.

✧

They swallowed the shame the world handed them — and smiled at home.

2025

The Second Beginning

✦

24 years after their wedding — they started over. Again. In Shadnagar.

✦

Nanna picked up his bag and went back to the roads he knew so well.

✦

Amma made yet another house feel like home — with nothing but her love.

✦

They didn't restart because they had to — they restarted because they never gave up.

2026

✦

The call that came that day — was the answer to every silent prayer they prayed alone.

✦

Somewhere, Amma cried tears she had saved for 25 years.

✦

✦

but because everything he sacrificed finally made sense.
